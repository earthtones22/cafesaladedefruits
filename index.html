About Us

Welcome to Cafe Salade de Fruits, nestled in the heart of the city, where we bring the timeless charm and authentic flavours of French bistro dining to your table. Our passion lies in crafting classic dishes using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Step into our warm, inviting atmosphere and let us transport you to the streets of Paris. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, weekend brunch, or simply a glass of wine, we strive to create unforgettable moments and delicious memories. Bon appétit!

We believe that dining is more than just eating; it's an experience. From the moment you walk in, our friendly staff aims to make you feel right at home, offering attentive service without being intrusive. Our chefs meticulously prepare each dish, focusing on quality and flavour, honouring traditional French techniques while embracing seasonal availability.

Join us and discover why Cafe Salade de Fruits has remained a beloved neighbourhood gem for years.
